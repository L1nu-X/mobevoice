Your number!

Calls that come from this number are recognized by Zenofon as belonging to your account. Add or edit a number that you call from and would like added to this account. Don't add public numbers, like a shared office or a hotel number. When you dial from those numbers use your PIN for identification instead.

Cost to call?

There is no extra charge for calls into the Zenofon system, but if you use callback to "%name%"this phone at %billing_number%, this number is located in '%billing_country_name% (%billing_country_code%)' . Zenofon bills it as '%billing_rate_name%' and our extra callback rate is $%billing_rate_per_minute% per minute. (Prices subject to change without notice) but call back will fail because we have no callback rate to this location. Either you didn't enter a number or you entered a wrong number. Enter a correct number and come back here to check the rate.
