SMS Help Page



Sl No Command Description
1 Zenofon To access main menu SMS ‘zenofon’ from your mobile. Zenofon reply with list of SMS services that you can use to manage your account.
2 Mute /Unmute Mute and Unmute services provides zenofon customers to activate and deactivate SMS services to their mobile number. User can SMS ‘Mute’ to deactivate or ‘Unmute’ to activate SMS Services.
3 Add New You can add new contact to your zenofon contact list via SMS.
To add new contact
  • SMS 'add new' to zenofon.
  • SMS ‘Contact name, Contact number’. For international number add 011.
Zenofon will send you corresponding USA number for added contact. Save this number in your phone and when you want to call overseas, just dial this USA number.
4 Reroute Zenofon lets you to choose rate value for your contact. All you have to do is:
  • SMS ‘reroute’ to zenofon. Our system will reply back you to select the contact you wished to change the route.
  • SMS the dial number[corresponding USA number] or contact name back to zenofon. Then you will receive a SMS to select the route number from list of route values.
  • SMS r[route number] [For example r1,r2,…,r9 etc] and you will receive a confirmation SMS that route number has changed for the selected contact.
5 Call History This feature allows user to know the details of last 3 most recent calls made by user. Just SMS ‘call history’ you will receive the call details of last three calls.
6 Last Call SMS ‘last call’ to know the details of most recent calls made by you.
7 Refund This feature allows you to request for the last call amount to be refund to your account. Zenofon admin will review your request and allow you a full or partial refund.
8 Next/Next2 ‘Next’ and ‘Next2’ command let you know the second and third last call made from your Zenofon number.
9 Refer Friend You can invite a friend to join zenfon, and you will get a credit when they make test call and when they recharge.
To refer a friend
  • SMS ‘refer friend name, friend number.
  • Zenfon will send an invitation to your friend number.
10 Add You can add multiple number as calling number to your account. To add new calling number SMS add [new user number] to Zenofon.
11 English To set your message language as english SMS 'English' to zenofon.
12 Spanish To set your message language as spanish SMS 'Spanish' to zenofon.
13 French To set your message language as french SMS 'French' to zenofon.
14 Help SMS help to get help instructions.
15 Reroute Last Call You can change the route number for last incomplete call.
  • SMS ‘reroute last call’ , Zenofon reply back to you to enter the route number.
  • SMS r[route-number], Zenofon change the route value for the last called number
16 Pin To request your pin number SMS PIN to Zenofon.
17 Change Pin Zenofon will change your pin number upon your request.
To change pin
  • SMS ‘changepin’. Zenofon reply back you to enter the last four digit of your credit card number for authentication.
  • SMS [last four digit of your credit card number].
  • Zenofon reply back to you with new pin number.
Imp: You should add credit card details in your Zenofon profile to access this service.
